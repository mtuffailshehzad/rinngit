                • Expertise/ Skills and attribute required.

                Master degree or above in relevant field, Active Researcher, Technology Orientation, DM and Climate Change related expertise.

                Possible utility in NIDM.

                Research supervision, Research projects execution, Capacity Building, Strategic planning.

              •   University Student/Research Scholar
                • Expertise/ Skills and attribute required.

                Research tools and techniques, Interpersonal skills, Team player, Gender sensitization, Comfortable with field work and community interaction.

                Possible utility in NIDM.
