              National Institude of Disaster Management (NIDM)

              (National Think Tank Disaster Management.)

              National think tank on Disaster Managment

              Build capacities of all stakeholders in the field of disaster management by developing their core competencies while creating information and knowledge base related to disaster risk reduction and management policies, strategies, mechanisms and practices.

              Human Resource Development stands amongst the most important areas for strengthening national resilience and to institutionalize Disaster Risk Reduction in Pakistan. Chapter VII of the National Disaster Management Act 2010 envisages establishment of National Institute of Disaster Management (NIDM). Accordingly NIDM, a statutory institution attached to National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA), was established to cater the training, research, advocacy and knowledge management needs of the public and private sectors in the field of Disasters Risk Management (DRM). Training and capacity building initiatives are conducted by NIDM aiming at building capacities of the stakeholders for disaster risk reduction, preparation .
